About the School

Labas Senior High School is committed to providing quality education and holistic development for students. Established in the heart of Santa Rosa City, the school continues to be a beacon of learning, discipline, and excellence.

Our Mission

To develop lifelong learners who are equipped with 21st-century skills, values, and knowledge through quality and accessible education, fostering a culture of excellence, integrity, and service to the community.

Our Vision

A leading senior high school that produces globally competitive graduates who are morally upright, socially responsible, and ready for higher education, employment, or entrepreneurship.
